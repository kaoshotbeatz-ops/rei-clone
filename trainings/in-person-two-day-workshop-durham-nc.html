IN-PERSON Two-Day Workshop – Durham, NC

  • Thursday, August 28, 2025 9:00 AM
    Friday, August 29, 2025 5:00 PM
  • St. Luke's Episcopal Church 1737 Hillandale Road Durham, NC, 27705 United States (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

Hosted by: Organizing Against Racism (OAR) Durham

REI’s two-day workshop is designed to develop the capacity of participants to better understand racism in its institutional and structural forms. Moving away from a focus on personal bigotry and bias, this workshop presents a historical, cultural, and structural analysis of racism. With shared language and a clearer understanding of how institutions and systems are producing unjust and inequitable outcomes, participants should leave the training better equipped to begin to work for change.
